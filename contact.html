Hey there! My name is Alexandra, and I'm a wedding photographer in the Austin area. I'm originally from Houston (I talk about it all the time), and I moved to San Marcos back in 2013 to get my BFA in photography. During my last semester of undergrad, I moved up to Austin to jumpstart my photography business. Almost two years after graduating and a year and a half of working a dead end job I hated, I decided to make my way back to Texas State to get a Master's in Mass Communication. Not sure if my love for the campus or my quarter life crisis brought me back here, but I'm so excited to be learning new things again. I love dogs, horchata with espresso, barbecue, and riding my bike without a helmet. I also love to travel, preferably via road trip.

As a grad student at Texas State, I've also recently gotten into media design. This site will be used to showcase my web projects, as well as my photography portfolio. To see my full photography site, click these words. Thanks for stopping by!
